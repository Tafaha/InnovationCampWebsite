When the teacher opens the app,
an overview of the classes will appear.
Let´s go ahead and choose
for example class "1B".


Here the teacher will have a full overview
of the students that are in the class.
The teacher can choose one student.
Let´s choose for example "Cullen Michael".


The teacher will be able to see
the students attendance calendar.
In this case the student has
been away for for than a month and
the posibilities of dropout can get higher.
Let´s pretend that the date today
is 31st of October and the student
isn´t attendant. Let´s press todays date.


The teacher will have to fill out an attendance form.
In this case we will have to choose "No"
because the student isn´t attendant
and comment the reason. Comment text fields with
no comments will increase
the dropout posibilities of the student.

All the information that has
been given by the teacher with the app,
will be transferred to our STAY database,
and will be shared with Redd Barna, and
the Uganda Bureau of Statistics. This data will
be used to make a virtual presentantion about
the physical world based on attendance.

After filling out the student attendance form,
and choosing no as to if the
student was attendant or not.
In this case we chose "No" and
the box with todays date turned red.
